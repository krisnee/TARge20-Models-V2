V-model: Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages Disadvantages
➕ Kanban is a very simple and easy to understand system that makes it practical for the management of a company to apply this system effectively. ➖ Kanban cannot be used as an independent tool. It is not a methodology that could be applied solely rather it can be merged with other processes and systems of a company like JIT, make to order and scrum etc. making these systems more visible.
The main advantage of applying the kanban system is a direct reduction in the costs and wastage of the company. As the tasks are continuously shifted between the columns of kanban board, the prediction of specific timelines for completion of tasks or activities becomes difficult.
Kanban system advocates continuous and sustainable improvements in the production systems of the company. Kanban is not suitable for the environments that are dynamic in nature.
