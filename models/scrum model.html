Scrum model: Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages Disadvantages
➕ Scrum model can help teams complete project deliverables quickly and efficiently. ➖ Scrum model often leads to scope creep, due to the lack of a definite end-date.
Scrum can help teams complete project deliverables quickly and efficiently. The chances of project failure are high if individuals aren't very committed or cooperative.
Large projects are divided into easily manageable sprints. The framework can be successful only with experienced team members.
